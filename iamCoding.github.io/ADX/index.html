ADDAX Electric, Inc. (formally JAYCOR Construction Technologies, Inc. / dba ADDAX Electric) was formed in 2012 and has been a licensed contractor since 1974. About Us

ADDAX Electric is headed by nearly 20 years of Electrical Systems experience & 40 years of General Engineering Construction experience. ADDAX Electric provides installation and service for government, state, city, county & private party sectors ranging from single family residences to shopping malls, multi-family apartment buildings and office buildings. In addition, we provide installation and services for schools, dormitories and churches.
